Project 04 - Nano Tank

Simulate the aquarium ecology, Let the user have a unique aquarium, and allow the user to interact with the aquarium elements.

Context

I am trying to working in the games sector of design. And make an interactive ecosystem. The inspiration from Tamagotchi and many computer games. Tamagotchi can feed pets . Computer games have diverse climate change.

General Concept

The concept was to transform basic natural data into the aquarium tank. And user can also control some elements of the aquarium tank.

Impetus

Aquariums need space and spend a lot of time taking care of fishes. It is not everyone can have an aquarium tank. But it is quite relaxing and relieve pressure when watching those fishes. So that is why I make a digital aquarium.

Goals

The goal of the "Nano Tank" is to simulate the real aquarium, show how the environment changes by real weather data.

Precedents

The right side reference shows the group of species system. It more like fishes swim together. The left side reference visual design is more like what I want.

Tools and Methods

I use photoresistor to get data of the brightness and transform to the brightness of the nano tank background color. Potentiometer to replace temperature sensor, so the temperature change can be more obvious in prototyping. Use press sensor as an air pump switch
